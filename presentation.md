# Social Yoddha 🦸‍♂️🌿
**Empowering Citizens to Become Warriors for their Neighborhoods**

---

## Problem Statement Selected

Lack of an engaging, community-driven platform for citizens to actively report, track, and collaborate on local civic issues (like fallen trees, waterlogging) in real-time, often leaving residents feeling disconnected from local problem-solving.

---

## Solution Overview

**Social Yoddha** is a modern, dynamic web platform that empowers citizens to become "Yoddhas" for their neighborhoods. 

It provides a premium, interactive UI to track local issues, form action teams, and collaborate in real-time to solve community problems effectively.

---

## Key Features

- **Dynamic Issue Dashboard:** View active civic issues on interactive cards with detailed progress timelines.
- **Community Teams & Real-Time Chat:** Join or create teams based on focus areas, and engage in a sleek, animated chat.
- **Express Agreements:** "Like to Agree" instantly updates a live counter badge.
- **Customizable Yoddha Profiles:** Personalize your profile with vibrant avatars and location tracking.

---

## Technologies Used

- **Frontend Framework:** React (via Vite)
- **Styling & UI:** Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion
- **Icons & Assets:** Lucide React, DiceBear API (Avatars)
- **Mapping:** React Leaflet
- **3D Elements:** React Three Fiber

---

## Google Technologies Utilized

- **Google Fonts:** Utilizing *Inter* and *Poppins* for a clean, modern, and highly legible typography system.
- *(Note: Ready for further integration with Google Cloud Run, Firebase, or Google Maps Platform for backend and scaling)*
